An Idea Was Born

COVID-19 has drastically changed the way we live, communicate, and do business. Alex Lu is the son of a small immigrant business owner and witnessed this firsthand, with his father struggling to find relief to save his business. After combing through endless documentation to find a grant, Alex became inspired to find a better solution, along with William Diao, who saw Alex's story and wanted to help. This led to the creation of MyGrant; a tool that assists businesses in accessing many types of grants through a simple questionnaire.
